About the Project

Project Overview

This project will be considerd as helpful for all the freshers who wanted to built their carrer in IT Sector but are confused & did not have any idea & experience about it.

This project will help them by introducing them all the the related IT Carrers, job profiles, on demand skills, providing free e-books, help them in finding jobs & internships, provide knowledge about trending courses which they learn for free from well experienced teachers, introducing them about the various IT Companies & much more...

Key Features

  • Feature 1: User will get time-to-time updates of On demand skills in the website.
  • Feature 2: Getting access to various free e-learning resources which they not know before.
  • Feature 3: Simple & easy to use interface for beginners to learn & interact with the help of this website.

Challenges and Learnings

Since we all know that now-a-days in IT Sector competition is very high due to very less openings(vacancies) provided by companies in this sector & crowd of tallented graduates makes struggle for others in tech sector.

Conclusion

This project is a career guide for IT freshers, providing key resources like skill trends, free e-learning materials, job opportunities, and course recommendations. Its user-friendly interface, built with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, ensures easy navigation. Despite the competitive IT job market, this platform helps beginners gain clarity and confidence to kickstart their careers efficiently.
